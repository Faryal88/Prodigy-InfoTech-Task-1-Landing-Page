Information Technology

Information Technology (IT) refers to the use of computers, networks, and other electronic devices to store, retrieve, transmit, and manipulate data. It encompasses a wide range of technologies and processes, including hardware (like servers and computers), software (like operating systems and applications), and networks (like the internet and intranets). IT is crucial in various fields, including business, healthcare, education,and entertainment, as it enhances communication, improves efficiency, and enables the automation of complex tasks.
